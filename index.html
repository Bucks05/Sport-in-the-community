Sport in the Community

Welcome to Na Piarsaigh

A visit to Na Piarsaigh Hurling and Football complex in its present stature boasts three senior playing fields, one floodlit and sandbased to facilitate winter activities, an underage pitch for youth development and all weather complex of four pitches shows the high club standard sought through year of development. An indoor facility of a hurling arena with a fully equiped gymnasium shows the ambitious forward thinking strategy. The hardworking efforts of committees over the years has been fruitful and no doubt future bodies will continue their efforts to enhance the present marvelous facility. The social aspects of the club are also recognised with the modernised bar/function hall available to its members. We are proud of our club facilities and applaud the support of members and friends over the years whose efforts enabled us reach the present high standard provided.

Why Join Us

At Na Piarsaigh we welcome everyone to our club the GAA can be enormous fun. You can gain great friends in the community from day one when you enter the Club. GAA can be something you could get stuck into for your life, could be traing teams after you stop playing might be your children who are playing and your up supporting them. But most of all the Club is not just a community its a Family. Its not all about playing either we have trips away for kids and for the adults. For example our U14 & U15 will be going to France this year for 5 days durning the summer holidays from school and lot of the parents are after booking in aswell. For adults we have bus days out so far this year we have one for Killarney and also one for West Cork. And for the playing side of it we have our senior hurlers playing up in Killkenny and will be staying the night above for a few drinks after the match. Once again its not just a Club its a community and a family.
